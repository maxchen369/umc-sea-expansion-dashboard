How scores are calculated

Use Custom Weight Mode to change assumptions, then open any heatmap cell with Explain Score to audit the contribution by factor.

Raw inputs

Country and UMC-specific factor scores use a 1-5 scale before conversion into a 0-100 strategy score.

Adjustable weights

Preset lenses remain available. Custom Weight Mode exposes every scoring factor with sliders from 0 to 30.

Normalization

Positive weights are normalized automatically so high total weight does not inflate final scores.

Execution risk

Execution risk is modeled as a negative adjustment. Higher execution risk reduces the country lens score and mode fit.

Mode adjustments

Each expansion mode applies different adjustments because foundry, advanced packaging, sales office, and customer engineering support have different requirements.

Methodology & assumptions

How to read the model

Scoring basis

Scores are preliminary strategic estimates, not investment advice. Country inputs use a 1-5 scale before weighted conversion to a 1-100 score.

UMC-specific model

The model is specific to UMC, Fab 12i, mature-node and specialty foundry demand, customer engineering support, and OSAT adjacency.

Separate lenses

Foundry, advanced packaging, sales office, and customer engineering support should not use the same weights because their capex, risk, and ecosystem requirements differ.

Capital intensity

Foundry expansion has much higher capital intensity and execution risk than sales office or customer support operations.

Support center definition

Customer support center means B2B technical support, FAE support, customer engineering support, and account operations, not consumer call center.

Data freshness

Cards show published date, retrieved date, data year, and source confidence. Placeholder sources should be replaced before formal use.
