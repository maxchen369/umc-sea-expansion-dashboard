Methodology & assumptions

How to read the model

Scoring basis

Scores are preliminary strategic estimates, not investment advice. Country inputs use a 1-5 scale before weighted conversion to a 1-100 score.

User assumptions

Users can change assumptions using sliders or scenario presets. Each change recalculates country ranking, heatmap cells, and strategy cards.

Comparable evidence

Case studies are used as comparable evidence, not direct proof that UMC should repeat the same investment.

Capital intensity

Foundry expansion has much higher capital intensity and execution risk than sales office or customer support operations.

Support center definition

Customer support center means B2B technical support, FAE support, customer engineering support, and account operations, not consumer call center.
